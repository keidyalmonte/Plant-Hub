Services

Light Levels

The Photoresistor reads the light levels present in the area and displays the current and previous light readings

Soil Moisture Levels

The STEMMA Soil sensor reads the moisture levels within the soil and displays the current and previous readings

Humidity & Temperature Levels

The DHT11 Humidity sensor reads both humidity and temperature and displays the current and previous readings

About

  • 2020-2021

    Our Humble Beginnings

    The Plant Hub is an educational plant monitoring system engineered to facilitate a hands-on academic experience between a student and a supervising educator. The goal is to aspire and cultivate innovative and productive learning spaces within a K-12 environment in order to form good habits and teach children about responsibility without the hazards that come with owning a high-maintenance plant. It reads light, humidity, temperature, and soil moisture levels to display to the users.
